Pac-man Off Xanax

  • High Concept: Anxiety Ridden Pac-Man
  • Player Experience Goals: Player should feel tense and anxious
  • Genre: Arcade
  • Game play: Plays like pac-man with the exception that you go faster and faster
  • Prototype goal: Prototype should be mildly infuriating
  • Features: Fast and arcadey with added frustration
  • Setting/Premise: Pac-man's dot dungeon
  • Story: You bring your own interpretation
  • Target Audience: People who enjoy classic arcade games and have an intense dislike of their own cognitive faculties
  • Play time: Potentially limitless with the caveat that the longer you play the worse it reflects on you as a person
  • Strategies: Collect the dots and hit increasingly small timing windows for turns
  • Prototype Assets: Richard Davey - Phaser Coding Tips, How to Move Like PacMan
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: pac-man!
